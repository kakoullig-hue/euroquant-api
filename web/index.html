Data processing terms (summary)

Documents submitted in private mode are processed exclusively in volatile memory for the duration of the analysis and are irreversibly destroyed upon completion. No document, extract, or derivative copy is written to persistent storage at any point. Reports are generated from the analysis output only.

Submitted documents should be redacted or synthetic at this engagement level. Analysis of unredacted confidential material is available under a signed Data Processing Agreement — request it by email. Reports are advisory in nature and do not constitute legal advice or a regulatory determination.
